MyBaller

Myballer致力于在中国推广青少年体育运动，并在全球范围内推广中国的青年人才。
我们通过特别设计的社交媒体应用程序来做到这一点，它允许业余体育赛事和俱乐部创建线上/线下运动社区，建立故事情节，并让年轻的体育明星们成为明星。 通过我们的应用，我们正在创造一个高功率的生态系统，使年轻运动员能够在地方、国家和国际各级推广自己。

MyBaller

MyBaller is dedicated to promoting youth sports in China and exporting China’s youth talent on a global scale.
We do this through our specially designed social media app that allows amateur sports tournaments and clubs to create online/offline sports communities, build storylines and highlight up and coming young sports stars. Through our app we are creating a high-powered eco-system that empowers young athletes to promote themselves at local, national and international levels.
